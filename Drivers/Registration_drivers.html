Registration for drivers

To carry out the registration process, you must first upload your documentation..

1. First phase:

Personal information

If you are Honduran: Photo of your national identity document.

If you are a foreigner: Photo of your passport.

Selfie photography: Stay in the center and look at the camera. The face must be uncovered, without glasses, caps or mask

Bank data

Photo verifying your bank account

2. Second phase:

Bank data

Photo of your National Numerical Tax Registry (RTN)

Vehicle data

Photo of your driver's license
